Tinga

Description

You will be making Tinga. Yummy!

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breast halves
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 jalapeño pepper, seeded & chopped
  • 1/4 medium onion, chopped
  • 8 corn tortillas, warmed
  • 1 cup canola oil

Steps

  1. Place chicken in large saucepan with enough water to cover. Boil, and cook chicken until tender. Remove from heat, drain and cool.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of canola oil in skillet over medium heat. Saute tomatoes, jalapeños and onions until soft. Remove bones, shred and throw in skillet with tomatoes and peppers. Fry for a few minutes.
  3. Heat cup of canola oil in clean skillet over medium-high heat. Fill each tortilla with 1/4 cup of chicken mix, roll into cylinder. Fry tortillas in hot oil until golden, turning once. Drain on paper, and serve with sour cream / salsa / both.
