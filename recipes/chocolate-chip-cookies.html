Homemade Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

You will be baking delicious homemade chocolate chip cookies. The edges are crispy while maintaining a chewy middle.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 C)
  2. Cream together butter & sugar until smooth. Beat eggs one at a time then stir in vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter with salt. Stir flour, chocolate chips and nuts. Drop mix onto ungreased pans.
  3. Bake for 10 min / until edges are brown
