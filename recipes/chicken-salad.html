Chicken Salad

Description

You will be making a chicken salad. Easily feeds the family.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups chopped, cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup blanched silvered almonds
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped

Steps

  1. Toast almonds in frying pan over medium-high heat. Shake frequently as they burn easily.
  2. In medium bowl, mix mayo, lemon juice and pepper. Toss with chicken, almonds and celery.
